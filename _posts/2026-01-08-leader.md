Home River Media Oy is implementing the project *“Home River Media: Strengthening Business Operations and Networks”*.

The aim of the project is to strengthen the company’s business capabilities and international networking within the film industry. The project includes the preparation of a business plan and an international networking strategy. The project is co-funded by the European Union.
